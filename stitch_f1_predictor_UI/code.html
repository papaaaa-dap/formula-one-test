Our Philosophy

Data is the Soul of Speed

In Formula 1, intuition is a luxury we can no longer afford. Every millisecond, every degree of tire temperature, and every liter of fuel is a variable in an infinite equation of performance.

Apex Velocity was born from the belief that transparency in telemetry creates a more profound connection between the sport and its observers. We bridge the gap between the pit wall and the grandstand.

By utilizing high-fidelity machine learning, we translate the mechanical chaos of a Grand Prix weekend into actionable, predictable intelligence.
